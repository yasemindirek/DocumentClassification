 BBC News NI outlines the latest data on coronavirus and Covid-19 vaccinations across Northern Ireland and the Republic of Ireland. Six new Covid-19-related deaths have been reported in Northern Ireland on Friday. The total number of deaths linked to Covid-19 in Northern Ireland since the start of the pandemic is 3,329. Deaths are measured by recording those who died within 28 days of receiving a positive result in a test for coronavirus.  Another 1,218 cases of coronavirus were reported on Friday, down from 1,461 cases on Thursday. On Friday, there were 515 people with Covid-19 in hospital in Northern Ireland and five patients were in intensive care. On Thursday, there were 522 people with Covid-19 in hospital in Northern Ireland and six patients were in intensive care. Last updated 1 April at 14:15 BST Source: Department of Health Northern Ireland A total of 3,757,322 vaccines for Covid-19 have been administered in Northern Ireland as of Friday. A total of 1,423,833 people had had their first dose and 1,331,316 had had their second dose. Meanwhile, 20,607 third doses have been administered.  A total of 981,566 booster jabs have been administered, as of Friday. Last updated 1 April at 14:15 BST Source: Department of Health Northern Ireland There are 1,468 people in hospital with Covid-19 in the Republic of Ireland as of the latest figures on Friday, down from 1,535 on Thursday. There are 59 patients with Covid-19 in hospital intensive care units on Friday, up from 58 on Thursday. The total number of Covid-19-related deaths since the start of the pandemic is 6,786 as of Friday. This figure, which is subject to revision, includes "probable and possible" Covid-19-linked deaths. Another 5,750 PCR test-confirmed cases of coronavirus have been reported in the Republic of Ireland on Friday. In addition, 5,089 people registered a positive antigen test. Last update: 1 April at 16:00 local time Source: Department of Health Ireland A total of 7,838,256 vaccines for Covid-19 (excluding boosters) had been administered in the Republic of Ireland as of Thursday. A total of 3,829,659 people have had their first dose and 3,768,612 have had their second dose, while 239,985 single-dose vaccines have been administered. A total of 2,910,747 booster jabs had been administered as of Thursday. Last updated: 1 April Source: Department of Health Ireland
